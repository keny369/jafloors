Capabilities

What we deliver

Organised around environment suitability and commercial buyer needs — not a list of generic trade services.

01

Substrate Preparation

Grinding, levelling, floor strip-outs, moisture management and surface correction — the foundation every finish depends on.

Where it fits — Required on nearly every commercial project. Includes ride-on stripping for floor strip-outs, mechanical grinding, and precision levelling.
Why it matters — Adhesion failure and finish defects trace back to inadequate preparation.
Environments — All sectors.

02

Vinyl, Rubber and Wall Sheet Systems

Sheet vinyl, safety vinyl, rubber and wall sheet for environments where hygiene, durability and cleanability are core requirements.

Where it fits — Healthcare, aged care, kitchens, corridors, wet areas.
Why it matters — Welded seams, coved skirtings and wall sheet integration separate performance from failure.
Trust signal — JA Floors is a certified Altro installer for hygienic flooring and wall sheet systems.

03

Resin and Protective Coating Systems

Epoxy, polyurethane and specialist coatings for environments needing chemical resistance and traffic durability.

Where it fits — Kitchens, plant rooms, labs, service corridors.
Why it matters — Wrong system or incorrect application leads to delamination and premature failure.

04

Concrete Finishes and Durable Surface Treatments

Polished concrete, grind and seal, micro-topping, Architop and decorative surface treatments selected for operational suitability.

Where it fits — Retail, foyers, warehouse, service corridors. Decorative finishes like micro-topping and Architop increasingly specified in office fit-outs.
Why it matters — Done poorly, concrete dusts, stains and degrades. The system must suit the traffic and cleaning regime.

05

Hygienic Wall Sheeting Systems

High-impact hygienic wall sheeting for healthcare, kitchen, laboratory and controlled environments where walls need the same cleanability and durability as floors.

Where it fits — Hospitals, aged care, commercial kitchens, clean rooms, laboratories and any environment where wall surfaces must withstand impact, moisture and chemical cleaning.
Why it matters — Standard painted walls degrade under cleaning regimes and impact. Hygienic wall sheeting provides a seamless, impervious surface that maintains its integrity over years of service.
Trust signal — Certified Altro installation adds assurance on detailing, junctions and long-term hygienic performance.
Environments — Healthcare, kitchens, clean rooms and laboratories.
