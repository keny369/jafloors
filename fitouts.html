Office Fit-outs and Refurbishment

Flooring that balances finish quality with program pressure — substrate preparation, product coordination, timing and handover readiness.

Environment Challenges

What fit-out and refurbishment floors have to handle

  • Tight program timelines — flooring often sits on the critical path
  • Substrate condition unknown until strip-out is complete
  • Coordination with joinery, mechanical, electrical and other trades
  • Occupied building works — noise, access and after-hours constraints
  • Handover readiness and surface protection through to practical completion
